Triconville is the Creator of Moments at IFEX 2025

The 2025 edition of IFEX Jakarta has been an exciting experience. Scheduled from March 6 to 9 at the Jakarta International Expo (JIExpo), this event promises to be a pivotal gathering for professionals and enthusiasts alike. Indonesia is one of the largest markets for natural material-based furniture, with manufacturers offering products made from teak wood, rattan, wicker, hardwood, and more. IFEX 2025 highlighted the skills of local artisans who blend traditional craftsmanship with modern design, reinforcing Indonesia’s strong presence in the global furniture industry.

Looking back at IFEX 2025, we are truly honored to have had the opportunity to showcase our dedication to outdoor design and innovation. As the creators of moments, Triconville design is thoughtfully curated to tell a story—one that reflects our commitment to crafting outdoor spaces in a balanced and respectful synergy with the natural surroundings. Walking through the booth, visitors not only discovered the outdoor trends but also connected with our vision of creating spaces where design, comfort, and nature come together in perfect harmony. The event was a hub of inspiration and idea-sharing, offering hands-on experiences, and we are excited to continue exploring new possibilities for the future of outdoor living.

In 2025, we continue our quest to showcase our premium teak while redefining how it seamlessly blends with ceramics, rope, GFRC, and powder-coated aluminum—unlocking endless design possibilities.

Keep an eye out for updates on our website, and if you haven’t yet, subscribe to our newsletter to stay connected.

We loved connecting with designers, hearing different perspectives, exchanging visions, and exploring future projects reminded us why we do what we do. It’s moments like these that keep us inspired to create.
