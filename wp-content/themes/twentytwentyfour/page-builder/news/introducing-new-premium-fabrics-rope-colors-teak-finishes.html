Olefin Fabric

From design & durability to comfort, olefin offers the best solution of outdoor project. Resilient, colorfast, stain-resistant and durable, it lends itself to a wide variety of applications. Outdoor fabric shouldn't fade in the sun, this is the consideration we bear in mind by choosing olefin. This fabric resist the degrading effects of long-term sunlight exposure and can be cleaned with bleach without compromising their color richness.
