#Outstanding design and process makes Gera Tables one of a kind;

In the production of Gera Tables, we select only premium mature teak logs that have the highest quality and desired lengths. These two parameters are a must have when logs are regulated, ordered and prepared.

A lot of preparation goes into these tables. A precise solid cut of 45mm is kept into kiln-dry for 3 to 4 weeks to maintain a moisture content level below 12%. The slats are well-sanded to get the beautiful teak texture to life. The blend of powder-coated legs mixes very well with the nature of the design. To save the tables for minor cracks over a period, Teak Shield is applied; a transparent coating layer that makes the Tabletop water repellent.

Material & Care

Teak furniture is an outstanding material for outdoor living. While the aluminum base is well-protected from corrosion from long exposure to the weather change. Only premium grade plantation grown teak is used for our teak furniture collection. Contains high natural oil, it is highly resistant to rotting and most impervious to the effects of sun, rain, frost and snow. Use a dry cloth to dust furniture weekly. Upon spill, please clean it using a fairly amount of mild soap, scrub accordingly to remove grease areas, followed by a rinse with clean water. Furniture should be thoroughly cleaned regularly and always before storing.
