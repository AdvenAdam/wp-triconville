Furniture exhibition at Swiss trade show in zurich, Triconville showcased high-end teak and stainless steel collections in International furniture design fair that took place in Zurich, Switzerland.  These collections fully convey creativity, research and innovation reflecting the values of triconville.

These collections were designed to keep the outdoor culture and feel alive. We offered our perception of living the outdoor life and created distinct collections of seating, tables, loungers and gazebos with materials used as natural teak and stainless steel. This allowed us to create relaxing outdoor areas with exclusive ranges of designs to reshape the outdoor space to reﬂect the beauty of the nature.
