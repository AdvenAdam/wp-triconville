20 Years of Excellence Experience on Outdoor Living

Born with a simple reason, a shortage of high quality furniture in the market. Triconville company decided to fulfill the demand, and improve the manufacturing process and practices to reach the level of comfort and quality.
How it Started

Triconville began the journey with an urge to create a beautiful piece of furniture with the finest quality at a very nice price. This induced us to collaborate with the designers, architects, and home lovers to bring their best designs to our customers. Choosing the best from their designs and combining it with world-class material quality and craftsmanship, to create a new lifestyle that brings people together in style and comfort.

since 2003

Our expertise is based on a deep knowledge of craftsmanship, and is at the origin of our manufacturing skill. Our design stems from a constant comparison with the most evolved stylistic scenarios, as befits a brand deeply rooted in contemporary desires.

“There is nothing more gorgeous than having an outdoor open space with cozy seating”

This drives us to create a quality outdoor furniture product that is intimately connected to nature. Keeping the culture and feel alive, we offer Triconville Product Ranges that include distinct collections of sofas, chairs, tables, loungers, parasols, and gazebos especially designed to reshape the outdoors to reflect the beauty of the nature.

The Triconville Milestone

2004 - 2008

Debuted the first outdoor teak collection. The collection had a positive feedback and quality was much appreciated by the dealers and traders.

  • Triconville enlarged the product range to stainless steel and wicker. During this year, they started to export their furniture on a small scale.
  • The company strengthened the export presence by creating high quality outdoor furniture collections and expanded export to European regions.

    • 2009 - 2012

    Added traditional powder coating section, to offer different colors to aluminium collections.

  • Triconville launches the Aluminum powder coated collection, the first contemporary outdoor furniture design made of batyline,aluminum and teak wood.
  • Integrated ERP systems with our Business model.
  • Setup of Triconville Malaysia Business.

    • 2013 - 2017

    Demand of increasing working space resulted in opening its 2nd manufacturing plant in Demak, Indonesia.

  • Added fully automated powdercoating section.
  • Added CNC laser-cut machine to fasten and improve the precision of working with metal sheets.
  • New outdoor collections that are all a clear commitment to innovation and versatile designs.
  • Integrated Inventory, logistics and warehouse management to our ERP systems to function efficiently.

    • 2018 - 2022

    Triconville has streamlined the production lines by adding more CNC machines to increase the efficiency on the ground and increased the working space.

  • We strive to be your one-stop supplier for Home and Living products, hence present to you a new trade showroom in Jepara, of about 6000 m².
  • Triconville brings you exclusive Product Ranges that will give your home an on-trend design update. Furthermore, these ranges are easily combined to create an integrated style indoors & outdoors.
  • Added CNC woodworking in the production workspace.
  • Added Upholstery & Cushion factory unit to support the highest quality of craftsmanship.
  • Introducing Trivonville B2B Portal for speedy order processing, downloadable digital assets, and product-rich information pages.
