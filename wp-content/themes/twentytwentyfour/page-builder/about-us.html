Crafting Outdoor Spaces with Passion and...

At Triconville, we're more than just outdoor furniture designers : we're creators of moments. We design outdoor furniture because we love it. Each piece is crafted not just to be beautiful, but to bring people together. When the sun is shining, and the ones you care about are gathered around, you feel the connection – that’s the feeling we aim to inspire with every product.

Rooted in Indonesia’s rich artisanal heritage, Triconville crafts outdoor furniture that blends Indonesian craftsmanship, sustainability, and modern innovation. We source responsibly, minimizing our environmental impact, while creating pieces that last for generations.

Our outdoor furniture seamlessly finds its home in contemporary spaces across the globe, while remaining authentic and full of soul. No matter what your style is, our diverse mood boards offer inspiration for your perfect outdoor sanctuary.

And by today, Triconville brings a touch of heritage and global sophistication to patios and outdoor spaces worldwide.

Handcrafted Heritage For Modern Living

Our Indonesian artisans blend time-honored techniques with modern design, meticulously crafting each piece by hand. This dedication to detail ensures that every piece of furniture is as unique as the environment it inhabits. We select premium materials like teak and powder-coated aluminum for their beauty, strength, and sustainability.

Our passion for innovation drives us to explore new techniques, ensuring that our furniture is not only crafted with heritage but also designed for many years to come.

Design That Speaks To The Soul

Our expert team finds inspiration in the world around us – the rich tapestry of cultures, the tranquility of nature, and the ever-evolving needs of modern living.

This global perspective allows us to create furniture that resonates with diverse tastes and lifestyles, bringing harmony to any outdoor space.

The Earth Jewel — Our Signature Teak

Teak is the heart and soul of Triconville. We work with only the finest materials. Its natural warmth, uniqueness, durability, and resistance to the elements make it the perfect choice for outdoor living. It’s a material that gets better with time, weathering elegantly to give your one-of-a-kind outdoor space look a sense of timeless beauty.

    Our Milestones:

    A Journey Through Time

    2003

    A group of friends in Indonesia shared a dream: to craft outdoor furniture that would transform ordinary gatherings into lasting memories by marrying style, comfort, and durability.

    Triconville’s debut teak collection quickly captivated European buyers, introducing the world to our unique blend of Indonesian craftsmanship and modern appeal.

    2004

    2009

    Embracing innovation, Triconville pioneered contemporary designs with aluminum and batyline.

    Triconville expanded its manufacturing capabilities and explored new design possibilities, broadening its range of outdoor furniture styles.

    2013 - 2015

    2017

    Deepening connection to worldwide customers through a user-friendly B2B portal.

    Triconville expanded globally, introducing its premier outdoor furniture to new markets and opening a trade showroom in Jakarta.

    2019-2021

    2023

    Continuing to innovate, Triconville invested in technology and talent to create holistic outdoor experiences that go beyond furniture.

    Triconville opened trade showrooms in Saudi Arabia.
