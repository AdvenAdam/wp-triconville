20 Years of Excellence Experience on Outdoor Living

Born with a simple reason, a shortage of high quality furniture in the market. Triconville company decided to fulfill the demand, and improve the manufacturing process and practices to reach the level of comfort and quality.
How it Started

Triconville began the journey with an urge to create a beautiful piece of furniture with the finest quality at a very nice price. This induced us to collaborate with the designers, architects, and home lovers to bring their best designs to our customers. Choosing the best from their designs and combining it with world-class material quality and craftsmanship, to create a new lifestyle that brings people together in style and comfort.

since 2003

Our expertise is based on a deep knowledge of craftsmanship, and is at the origin of our manufacturing skill. Our design stems from a constant comparison with the most evolved stylistic scenarios, as befits a brand deeply rooted in contemporary desires.

“There is nothing more gorgeous than having an outdoor open space with cozy seating”

This drives us to create a quality outdoor furniture product that is intimately connected to nature. Keeping the culture and feel alive, we offer Triconville Product Ranges that include distinct collections of sofas, chairs, tables, loungers, parasols, and gazebos especially designed to reshape the outdoors to reflect the beauty of the nature.
