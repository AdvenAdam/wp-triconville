R&D Head

full time - Semarang, Indonesia

overview

We are currently seeking a R&D Head to help us develop innovative indoor furniture manufacture. Your mission is to launch new products that stand out from the competition and drive significant new revenue growth.

responsibility

    Product Strategy :

  • Analyze sales figures, customer reactions, market trends, and changes to existing products.
  • Recommend new products by identifying market opportunities and filling product gaps.
  • Develop product pricing and positioning strategies.
  • Transform product strategy into clear product briefs with detailed requirements that solve meaningful customer problems.


    • Product Design & Development :
  • Translate product briefs into product designs, specs, and prototypes, with a strong sense of unique selling points and visually attractive aesthetics.
  • Build a best-in-class design and development process to accelerate and expand our product offering while strengthening our brand identity.
  • Collaborate with Product Development Team on the execution of all product development and costing activities from ideation to a final manufacturing-ready package.


    • Product Launch :
  • Manage successful execution of all go-to-market activities.
  • Drive workstreams in Content, Sales, Support, Inventory, Website, and Promotion as the product expert and the customer advocate.
  • Collaborate with Marketing and Creative teams to define the key product message that will be the primary selling point on the website, search, social media, and advertising.


Qualification

  • 5+ years experience in the indoor furniture industry.
  • 5+ years experience in product management, product development, retail merchandising, or a related.
  • 2+ years of experience scaling new product categories in a B2B consumer products brand.
  • Proven track record of managing all aspects of a successful product through its lifecycle, with an understanding of supply chain and sourcing processes.
  • A collaborative leader, leading by example and encouraging team members to innovate, improve and solve customer needs.
  • Impeccable taste and eye for visual design and practical product design.
  • Knowledge of current market, design, and product trends.
  • Experience with furniture and related categories.
  • Adaptable, with a hungry-to-grow mindset.
  • Resourceful, proactive, and a true team player.
  • Fluent in English is a plus.
  • Willing to visit the factory 2 times a week
