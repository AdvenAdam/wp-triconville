Upholstery & Cushion Head

full time - Jepara / Kudus, Indonesia

overview

Handles the renovation and repair of upholstered furniture, replacing fabrics, fillings, furniture components, restoring furniture, as well as creating custom upholstery pieces.

responsibility

  • Ensures sufficient stock of required materials/fabrics and supplies are in stock.
  • Repair or replace materials/fabrics.
  • Replace backrest and cushion covers.
  • Replace foams and repair backrest frames.
  • Monitor inventory and request supplies as needed.
  • Responsible for the repair and maintenance of all upholstered material, furniture, and finishes.
  • Execute projects and tasks as assigned and dispatched.


Qualification

  • Knowledge of tailoring, stitching, carpentry is a must.
  • Good practical, operational, interpersonal skills with a flair of creativity are a must.
  • At least 5 years’ experience as an Upholsterer.
  • At least 5 years’ experience stripping and installing new layouts on all table and repairing or re-upholstering chairs, sofas, and upholstered wall panels.
  • Working knowledge of all phases of upholstery repair and renewal.
  • Read and comprehend required documents.
  • Willing to be placed in a branch of our factory.
