overview

Furniture designers are responsible for the look and feel of the furniture we use daily. They’re tasked with creating new aesthetically pleasing and functional pieces while ensuring that these designs can be mass-produced at a reasonable cost. Furniture designers must have an eye for detail and an understanding of human anatomy and physiology. They must also work well in a team environment—they rely on engineers, woodworkers, metal workers, fabricators, etc., to help bring their ideas to life.

responsibility

  • Prepare technical drawings, section and details as per requirement.
  • Perform drafting and design work.
  • Ensure all drawings are in accordance with the standards set by company’s quality.
  • Rectify and update latest drawing accordance to changes.
  • Able to undertake any other duties assigned.
  • Determine and develop user requirements for systems in production, to ensure maximum usability.
  • Carry out research to help develop ideas.
  • Produce new designs, or improve existing ones.
  • Creating sketches and drawings of new designs using software such as AutoCAD or SolidWorks.
  • Meeting to discuss needs and preferences and recommending materials, colors, and styles that will meet their goals.
  • Take into account size, shape, ergonomics and production methods when producing design ideas.
  • Developing a thorough understanding of current trends in style so new designs will be desirable to consumers.
  • Reviewing existing furniture designs to identify opportunities for improvement or change.


Qualification

  • Candidate must possess at least Diploma/Advanced/Higher Graduate Diploma in Art/Design/Creative Multimedia or equivalent.
  • At least 2-5 year(s) of working experience in the related field is required for this position.
  • Hands-on experience with computer-aided design (CAD) software.
  • Familiarity with 3D modeling tools and solid works.
  • Good math skills.
  • Ability to meet deadlines- Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
