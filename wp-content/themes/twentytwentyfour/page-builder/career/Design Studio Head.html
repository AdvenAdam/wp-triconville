Design Studio Head

full time - Semarang, Indonesia

overview

Responsible for organizing all production needs and requirements ranging from design, photos, materials, marketing strategies such as content, product introduction to achieve the specified targets.

responsibility

  • Design and develop new furniture concepts in response to market trends, customer needs, and company objectives.
  • Research and analyze design trends, materials, production methods, and functional products.
  • Generate sketches, 3D models, and illustrations to communicate design ideas.
  • Collaborate with engineering, marketing, and manufacturing teams to refine designs and ensure feasibility of production.
  • Select furniture finishes, fabrics, and hardware based on aesthetic, functional, and safety requirements.
  • Manage projects from concept through delivery, ensuring that designs are executed flawlessly.
  • Conduct quality assurance testing to verify that products meet all safety and performance standards.
  • Plans, schedules, conducts, and manages various design projects, ensuring all initiatives adhere to acceptable design practices and compliance regulations.
  • Conducts idea mapping, design strategy, and journey maps to frame digital design initiatives.
  • Provides effective management of the Design Team to achieve desired results, ensuring the application of efficient work practices for core group processes.
  • Ensures that programs stay on track by resolving resource conflicts and removing obstacles, which may involve negotiations across teams. Assists with troubleshooting problems, discrepancies, and investigations of non-conformance.
  • Monitor customer feedback and modify designs accordingly.
  • Stay up-to-date on industry trends and developments.


Qualification

  • Professional 6 – 8 years (design/agency), 2+ years experience in art direction/managing/developing others or leading teams.
  • Proficient in AutoCAD, Rhino, 3ds Max, or similar software.
  • Strong record of collaboration, ensuring completion of projects/programs.
  • Bachelor’s degree in furniture design, industrial design, or related field.
  • 3+ years professional experience in furniture design.
  • Experience with woodworking, metalworking, and other fabrication techniques.
  • Experience working in a manufacturing or production environment.
