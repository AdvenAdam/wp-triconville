Sourcing Staff

full time - Jepara / Kudus, Indonesia

overview

We are currently seeking a Sourcing Staff. This role will be responsible for sourcing developments across Indonesia from design handoff to purchase orders. Analyze and deliver accurate pricing to a product that meets the original objectives.

responsibility

  • Managing the end-to-end sourcing and procurement of furniture across Indonesia.
  • Managing procurement activities for furniture & decor categories.
  • Identify potential vendors and develop a thorough understanding of the factory matrix strengths, weaknesses, competencies, quality levels, etc.
  • Establishing strategic relationships across vendor base and looking out for additional avenues of procurement with a key focus on cost and quality management.
  • Collaborate with QA/QC and Compliance teams to ensure our company standards are met.
  • Building and maintaining long-term relationships with vendors and suppliers.


Qualification

  • 3 years of experience in sourcing, product development, or related fields.
  • Knowledge of furniture construction and manufacturing is a plus.
  • Ability to work as part of multiple cross-functional teams.
  • Furniture development/sourcing experience is a must.
  • Potential travel up to 30% of the time
  • Experience in developing long-term supply relationships
  • Need very strong attention to detail and consistent follow-through.
