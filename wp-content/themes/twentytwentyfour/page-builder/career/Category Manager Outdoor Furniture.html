Category Manager Outdoor Furniture

full time - Semarang, indonesia

overview

The role of a Furniture Category Manager Outdoor Furniture involves overseeing and managing a specific category of furniture products within a company. This position requires a combination of strategic thinking, product knowledge, and market understanding.

responsibility

    Category Strategy Development :

  • Develop a comprehensive strategy for the furniture category, aligned with overall business objectives.
  • Identify growth opportunities, market trends, and competitive landscape within the furniture category.


    • Assortment Planning :
  • Work closely with suppliers and product development teams to curate a diverse and appealing assortment of furniture products.
  • Ensure that the assortment meets customer needs and aligns with the brand image.


    • Product Lifecycle Management :
  • Manage the entire lifecycle of furniture products, from introduction to retirement.
  • Monitor sales performance and recommend adjustments to the assortment based on market demand.


    • Market Research and Analysis :
  • Develop and implement pricing strategies for the furniture category.
  • Plan and execute promotions and discounts to drive sales and achieve revenue targets.


    • Cross-Functional Collaboration :
  • Collaborate with marketing, sales, and other departments to align category strategies with overall business goals.
  • Work with the marketing team to create effective promotional materials and campaigns.


    • Inventory Management :
  • Monitor and manage inventory levels to ensure optimal stock levels and prevent stockouts or overstock situations.
  • Implement inventory control measures to maximize efficiency.


    • Quality Control and Compliance :
  • Ensure that furniture products meet quality standards and comply with relevant regulations.
  • Work with suppliers to address quality issues and implement improvements.


    • Customer Feedback and Satisfaction :
  • Gather customer feedback and insights to understand their preferences and satisfaction with the furniture category.
  • Use customer data to make data-driven decisions on product offerings and improvements.


    • Sales Analysis and Reporting :
  • Track and analyze sales performance for the furniture category.
  • Prepare regular reports and presentations for management, highlighting key metrics and areas for improvement.


    • New Product Development :
  • Identify opportunities for new product development within the furniture category.
  • Work with product development teams to bring innovative and market-relevant products to market.


Qualification

  • Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Marketing, Merchandising, or a related field. A Master’s degree may be preferred.
  • Proven experience in a similar role, preferably in retail, furniture, or home goods industries.
  • Background in category management, product management, or merchandising.
  • Strong analytical skills to interpret data and market trends.
  • Creative thinking and the ability to curate an appealing assortment of furniture products.
  • Knowledge of furniture industry trends, materials, and manufacturing processes.
  • Customer-centric mindset with a focus on customer satisfaction and preferences.
