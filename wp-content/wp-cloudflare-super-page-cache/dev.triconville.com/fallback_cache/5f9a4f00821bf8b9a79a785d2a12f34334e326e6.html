SALONE DEL MOBILE MILANO 2024

We are delighted to announce that Triconville Brand will be present at Salone Del Mobile 2024 in Milan. As we know this annual fair trade attracts more than 20,000 visitors from all over the world and we are very excited to be part of the biggest event of the year!

 

Swing by our booth, pavilion 3 to get you insights and ideas on the latest trends in furniture and outdoor design.

 

Booth Pavilion 3 | Stand M28-M30

April 16-21 2024

Rho Fiera Milano spaces

 

 

 

The Salone del Mobile is a hub of experimentation and cross-pollination, a meeting point and a place for new opportunities to reflect on the world of design and planning. The 2024 edition will take place from 16th to 21st April 2024, in the Rho Fiera Milano spaces, as is traditional.
